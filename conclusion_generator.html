Frequently Asked Questions

How does the AI write a conclusion?

The AI reads the entire text you provide, identifies the key arguments, themes, and evidence, and then synthesizes this information into a coherent summary paragraph to effectively end your work.

Does it just repeat the introduction?

No. A good conclusion should not just rephrase the introduction. Our tool aims to create a true conclusion by summarizing the main points from the body of your text and often suggesting a final, impactful thought.

What is the ideal text length to input?

The tool works best with complete or nearly-complete drafts of essays, articles, or reports that are at least a few hundred words long. This gives the AI enough context to write a meaningful conclusion.
