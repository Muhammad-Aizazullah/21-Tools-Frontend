Frequently Asked Questions

How does the AI Essay Generator work?

You provide a topic, desired length, and tone. The AI then researches the topic, outlines a structure (introduction, body, conclusion), and writes a complete essay draft based on your requirements.

Is the generated essay plagiarism-free?

The content is generated uniquely for each request, making it original. However, it's designed as a writing assistant. We always recommend you check the content and, most importantly, add your own insights and proper citations.

Can I use this for my school assignments?

This tool is an excellent assistant for generating ideas, creating outlines, and drafting initial versions of your essays. You should always review and edit the content and adhere to your institution's academic integrity policies.
