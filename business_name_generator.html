Frequently Asked Questions

How does the AI generate business names?

Our AI combines your keywords with creative naming conventions, synonyms, prefixes, suffixes, and industry trends to create a diverse list of unique and brandable name ideas.

Are the generated names available to use?

The generator provides creative ideas. We strongly recommend that you check if your chosen name is available as a registered business name and domain in your country or region before using it.

Can I get names for any type of business?

Yes, the tool is highly versatile. The more specific and descriptive your keywords are, the more relevant and tailored the name suggestions will be for your specific niche or industry.
