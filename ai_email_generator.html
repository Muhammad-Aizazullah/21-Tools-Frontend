Frequently Asked Questions

How does the AI Email Generator work?

Our AI Email Generator uses advanced natural language processing (NLP) models to understand your inputs (recipient, subject, purpose/keywords) and generate a coherent, professional email. It analyzes the context and intent to craft appropriate phrasing, tone, and structure.

Can I customize the tone or length of the email?

Currently, the tool aims for a professional and informative tone. For length, you can be more specific in your "Email Purpose / Keywords" to indicate if you need a brief or detailed email. Future updates may include explicit tone and length options.
