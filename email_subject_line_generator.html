Frequently Asked Questions

Why is a good email subject line important?

The subject line is the first thing your recipients see. A good one dramatically increases open rates and engagement, while a bad one can cause your email to be ignored or, worse, marked as spam.

What makes an email subject line effective?

Effective subject lines are typically short, descriptive, and create a sense of urgency or curiosity. Personalization and relevance to the recipient are also key factors for success.

How many subject lines does the tool generate?

Our tool provides a variety of options with each request, giving you multiple creative ideas to choose from. This is perfect for A/B testing to see which subject lines resonate most with your audience.
