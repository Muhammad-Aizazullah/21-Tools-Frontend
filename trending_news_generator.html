Frequently Asked Questions

Where does the news come from?

The AI synthesizes information from recent and publicly available data to generate news summaries based on your keywords. It is designed to capture the essence of trending topics.

Is the generated news 100% real-time?

The tool is excellent for getting a quick overview of a topic, but it is not a direct report from a live news agency. Its knowledge is based on its last training data, so it may not have information on events that occurred in the last few hours.

Can I use this for professional news reporting?

This tool is best used for gaining a quick understanding of trends, generating content ideas, or for casual reading. For professional reporting, we always recommend consulting and citing primary, verified news sources.
